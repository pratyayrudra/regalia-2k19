About the event

Regalia,the annual cultural festival of RCC Institute of Information Technology,is the biggest celebration of music,art and dance in town.It is the place where students from all walks of life can come together and unleash their creative horses.  Like every year Regalia will be an amalgamation of the competitions and outstanding performances.Regalia 2019 promises to bring up to the audience the best experience ensuring it to be the most memorable one. Regalia for us is not only an event but a journey to define ourselves,find our ways and most importantly take back a lot from the team. So we call all of you to be a part of this celebration, make our journey bigger and gather this experience all by yourself because this time we are are DREAMING BIG,and we will LIVE BIGGER
